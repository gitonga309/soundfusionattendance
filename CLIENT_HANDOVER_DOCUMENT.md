## How to Use the System

### For Workers: Daily Workflow

#### 1. **Registration & Login**
- Visit the system URL
- Click "Register"
- Fill in personal information (name, email, phone, date of birth, etc.)
- Create password
- Confirm email
- Log in

#### 2. **Mark Attendance**
- Log in to the system
- Click "Mark Attendance"
- Select the event (if applicable)
- Click "Present"
- System automatically records the attendance

#### 3. **Log Overtime Hours**
- Click "Mark Attendance" again
- Enter overtime hours (0-24)
- System calculates payment automatically
- Example: Base 1000 KSH + (2 overtime hours √ó 100 KSH) = 1200 KSH

#### 4. **View Your Balance**
- Go to "My Balance" section
- See total unpaid earnings
- View breakdown by date

#### 5. **Edit Records (if needed)**
- Click on any record to edit
- Can edit once per day
- System updates your balance automatically

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues & Solutions

#### **"I forgot my admin password"**
- Contact your developer to reset it
- Keep credentials in a secure location

#### **"A worker can't log in"**
- Check that the worker's account was created
- Verify email confirmation was completed
- Try password reset
- Contact your developer if issue persists

#### **"Attendance not marking"**
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Use a different browser
- Contact support if still having issues

#### **"Balance calculation seems wrong"**
- Check that all records are marked as "paid" or "unpaid"
- Verify overtime hours entered are correct
- Review adjustment history
- Contact your developer to verify calculation logic

#### **"Can't access the system"**
- Check the correct URL with your developer
- Try using Chrome or Firefox
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Contact your developer if site is down
